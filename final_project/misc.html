Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

As the official sequel of Counter-Strike 1.6, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is one of my main entertainment. I play every weekend with another four teammates. Skillwise, I'm not in the elite level, but I enjoy the fun more than just winning.

Professional scenes are an essential part of this game. There are many clubs and organizations all around the world (most organizations have CS:GO branches as well as others like DOTA2 or Overwatch). Throughout the year, around 15 large events are held, including two "Major" sponsered by Valve themselves. I would watch most of them if time allows.
